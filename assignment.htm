Our Country Nepal

Nepal is a beautiful and culturally rich country located in South Asia, bordered by India to the south and China to the north. Known for its stunning landscapes, including the Himalayan mountain range and Mount Everest, Nepal is a popular destination for trekking and outdoor adventure. The country is also home to a diverse range of ethnic groups and languages, with Nepali being the official language. The rich history and traditions of Nepal can be seen in its many temples, festivals, and cultural practices, including the famous Kumari tradition, where a young girl is worshipped as a living goddess. Despite facing political and economic challenges in recent years, Nepal remains a unique and fascinating country with much to offer visitors and residents alike.
